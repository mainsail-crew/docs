## How You Can Help

Beyond financial support, there are many ways to contribute to Mainsail:

- **Join the Community:** Engage with other users and developers in our
[Discord](https://discord.gg/mainsail){:target="_blank"} or
[GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/mainsail-crew/discussions){:target="_blank"}.
- **Star the Repository:** Show your appreciation by starring the
[Mainsail GitHub repository](https://github.com/mainsail-crew/mainsail){:target="_blank"}.
- **Report Issues:** Found a bug or have a feature idea? Let us know on
[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/mainsail-crew/mainsail/issues?q=sort:updated-desc+is:issue+is:open){:target="_blank"}.
- **Contribute Code:** Check out our [Contributing Guide](development/contributing.md) and submit pull requests.
- **Spread the Word:** Share your experience on social media, in forums, or with fellow 3D printing enthusiasts.
